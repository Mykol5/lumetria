6 Dimensions of Analysis

The Creative Scoring Engine

Each dimension scored from 0 to 10 to produce an overall predicted ROAS score

bolt
Hook Strength 0-10

Analyzes the first 3 seconds — does the hook stop the scroll?

The first 3 seconds determine whether someone watches or scrolls.
favorite
Emotional Trigger 0-10

Measures the level of emotion — surprise, desire, FOMO, humor...

Purchasing decisions are emotional. A video that creates no emotion doesn't sell.
speed
Pacing & Editing 0-10

Analyzes editing speed, cuts, and visual dynamics of the video.

Poorly calibrated pacing loses attention within seconds.
call_to_action
CTA Clarity 0-10

Evaluates if the call-to-action is clear, well-positioned and compelling.

A vague CTA = clicks that never come. This is often what kills a creative.
visibility
Visual Quality 0-10

Rates image quality, lighting, composition, and on-screen text readability.

Meta favors high visual quality in its distribution algorithm.
timeline
Narrative Structure 0-10

Analyzes the narrative arc — problem, solution, social proof, CTA.

A strong narrative structure naturally guides the viewer toward purchase.

Report example: A creative scored 7.8/10 with a strong hook (9/10) but a weak CTA (5/10) → Lumetria recommends reworking the call-to-action before launch.
